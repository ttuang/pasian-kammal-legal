Privacy Policy

Pasian Kammal
Last Updated: February 22, 2026

1. Introduction

Pasian Kammal ("we", "our", or "the App") is a devotional application designed to help users read daily verses, reflect spiritually, and track personal growth.

The App functions primarily offline and does not require user accounts.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information Stored Locally

  • Favorites (saved verses)
  • Personal notes
  • Daily reflections
  • Streak progress
  • Notification preferences
  • Backup settings

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

B. Notifications

Notifications are scheduled locally on your device. No notification data is sent externally.

C. Backup & Restore

Backup files are generated locally. We do not receive or store backup files.

3. No Accounts

The App does not require registration, email addresses, or login credentials.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use standard platform services such as Expo, iOS, and Android services. The App itself does not transmit personal devotional content externally.

5. Data Security

Data is stored locally on your device. We recommend exporting backups regularly.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use indicates acceptance of changes.
