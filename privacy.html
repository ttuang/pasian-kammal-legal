Privacy Policy Pasian Kammal Last Updated: February 22, 2026 1. Introduction Pasian Kammal ("we", "our", or "the App") is a devotional application designed to help users read daily verses, reflect spiritually, and track personal growth. We respect your privacy. The App is designed to function primarily offline and does not require user accounts. 2. Information We Collect A. Information Stored Locally on Your Device The App stores the following data locally on your device: • Favorites (saved verses) • Personal notes • Daily reflections • Streak progress • Notification preferences • Backup settings This data is stored only on your device using secure local storage. We do not automatically collect, transmit, or store this data on any external server. B. Notifications If you enable daily notifications: • Notifications are scheduled locally on your device. • No notification data is transmitted externally. You may disable notifications at any time in the Settings section. C. Backup & Restore The App allows you to export and import devotional data. • Backup files are generated locally. • You choose where to save or share them. • We do not receive or store backup files. • Sharing backups is entirely under your control. You are responsible for protecting exported backup files. 3. No Accounts Pasian Kammal: • Does not require account registration. • Does not collect email addresses. • Does not require login credentials. • Does not use cloud-based accounts. All devotional content remains under your control on your device. 4. Third-Party Services The App may use standard platform services including: • Expo framework • Apple iOS services • Android services • Device notification systems • Device file system access • System sharing tools These services operate under their respective privacy policies. The App itself does not transmit personal devotional content externally. 5. Data Security Data is stored locally on your device. We cannot guarantee protection against: • Device loss • Theft • Device compromise • Accidental deletion • Operating system issues We recommend exporting backups regularly. 6. Children's Privacy The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because no accounts or personal information are collected, the App is suitable for general audiences. 7. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant updates will revise the "Last Updated" date. Continued use indicates acceptance of changes. 8.
